# Global-sales-analysis
## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Global Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze worldwide sales performance and generate business insights from transactional sales data.
The dashboard provides a complete analytical view of sales activities across multiple regions, customer segments, and product categories. It enables decision-makers to monitor performance metrics, identify business opportunities, and support data-driven strategic planning.

## Project Insights
1. The Consumer segment dominates sales with ~51.48% (6.51M) of total revenue.
2. Asia Pacific is the top-performing market (~31.98% / 4.04M), followed by Europe (~26% / 3.29M).
3. The company generated 12.64M sales across 178K product units sold globally.
4. Average delivery time is maintained at 3.97 days, with only 1,080 return orders.
5. Product profitability varies significantly, with some products generating more than 20K profit, while loss products record up to 10K losses.

## Final Conclusion
To further improve business performance, the company should focus on expanding sales strategies within the Consumer segment and strengthening its presence in Asia Pacific and Europe, which generate the highest revenue.
Special attention should be given to retaining top-value customers through targeted promotions and loyalty programs. At the same time, underperforming products should be reviewed to reduce losses and improve profitability.
Maintaining fast delivery performance and low return rates will also be essential to sustaining customer satisfaction and supporting long-term global sales growth.
